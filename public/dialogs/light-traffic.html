Better Bridgeway Builder

A Frogger style game where you help the frog cross the road and avoid getting squashed. Complete levels, earn grant money and install new infrastructure to improve the road for pedestrians, bicyclists, drivers, and commercial deliveries.

Level1 - Morning Sunshine on the Waterfront

Early morning in Sausalito and the frog wants to enjoy the waterfront walk in the morning sunshine.
Get to the sidewalk near The Trident or Scoma's without getting squashed.
Traffic is light, but watch out for the occasional car or truck.

Controls

Desktop: Use the arrow keys (← ↑ → ↓) to move the frog.
Desktop: Click on the screen where you want the frog to go.
Mobile: Touch where you want the frog to go.
